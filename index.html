About Us

Savoury.AI was born from a passion for simplifying home cooking, conceived by a team of innovative students at the Indian School of Business (ISB) in Hyderabad, Telangana. Our mission is to empower individuals to effortlessly create delicious and personalized meals using the ingredients they have on hand. We believe that everyone, regardless of their culinary expertise, should have the tools to enjoy the art of cooking. Savoury.AI is our commitment to making that vision a reality, one recipe at a time.
